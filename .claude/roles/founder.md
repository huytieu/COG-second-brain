# Role Pack: Founder

## Recommended Skills

| Skill | Why it matters for you |
|---|---|
| team-brief | Daily cross-reference of GitHub, Linear, Slack, and PostHog — full visibility across your entire team's output without chasing updates |
| comprehensive-analysis | Deep 7-day analysis for board prep, investor updates, and strategic planning — pulls from every data source |
| meeting-transcript | Capture investor meeting commitments, board decisions, and team alignment outcomes with structured ownership |
| daily-brief | Market intelligence, competitor moves, fundraising news, and industry trends — personalized to your space |
| braindump | Capture strategic pivots, investor feedback, and cross-functional observations with automatic domain classification |
| weekly-checkin | Cross-domain pattern analysis — connects product progress to market timing to team dynamics |
| knowledge-consolidation | Build strategic frameworks, pitch narratives, and decision logs from scattered founder thoughts |
| url-dump | Save competitor launches, market analyses, and investor content with auto-extracted insights |
| onboarding | Initial setup — run this first |
| update-cog | Keep your COG framework current |

## Recommended Integrations

| Integration | Why it matters for you |
|---|---|
| github | Engineering velocity, release cadence, and technical health — essential for founder visibility |
| linear | Sprint progress, team blockers, and priority alignment — enables two-way sync from team-brief |
| slack | Cross-functional discussions, team sentiment, and decision trails across all channels |
| posthog | Product-market fit signals — activation funnels, retention curves, and feature adoption |
| notion | Company wiki, investor updates, and strategic documents for richer analysis context |
| hackmd | Collaborative notes from team meetings and strategy sessions |

## Suggested Agent Mode

`team` — Founders juggle the most context. Team mode delegates research across data sources and produces the comprehensive analysis founders need for decision-making.
